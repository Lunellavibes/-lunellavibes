Fresh Restart Ritual: Serenity & Magnetic Wealth

Light a candle in a soft tone (white, beige or gold). Play gentle, cleansing sounds (water, crystal bowls, soft jazz). Close your eyes. Take three slow breaths — in through the nose, out through the mouth. With every exhale, imagine tension melting from your body. With every inhale, welcome a wave of freshness, like morning air in early spring. In your mind (or whisper softly), repeat: I release what no longer serves me. I wipe the slate clean — with peace, elegance and ease. I am a receptive vessel for new beginnings, fresh energy and abundant money flow. Visualise a gentle golden light pouring into the crown of your head, washing down your spine like warm shimmer. See it spreading into your arms, chest, belly and legs — until your entire aura is glowing with liquid gold. Hold this vision. When you can feel yourself softly glowing, say: Money reaches me with ease. Wealth loves my calm magnetism. I lead a life of overflow, beauty and quiet confidence. Open your eyes slowly. Smile a little — as if you already know something good is on its way. Drink a glass of water with lemon or mint to seal the freshness.
